Ayas: Responsive Website Development

The redesign and development of the Ayas website is one of my favorite projects. The original site has many issues with functionality, usability, and accessibility, and was originally created before websites needed to worry about being responsive. The issues listed above were hurting the company's bottom line, as their pages didn't rank in any search results. The redesign has allowed the company to increase their daily page visits by 300%, and their sales have increased by 25% since the site was deployed.

Heiendo Studio: Responsive Website Redesign

The Heiendo Studio website was usable and functional, but did not properly communicate the studio's modern aesthetic. The redesign focused on building something that would give users online the same feelings the studio's store location did for those that visited in person. The clean lines of their goods are reflected in the page elements, and color is kept to a minimum in order to give the photos a chance to shine. Interactive protptypes helped the client to see and experience the new website before a single line of code was written.

Instagram Customer Journey Mapping

As part of my capstone project, I researched the Instagram app's usability. I began by studying the site to ensure that I understood which interactions might be causing pain points for users, then I mapped out three different customer journeys through the site, including the onboarding process and the sharing process. The final deliverable was an interactive prototype of the site build with Adobe Xd that included the interactions that I redesigned based on user testing and feedback.
